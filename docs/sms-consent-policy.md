# Mile High Home Finders — SMS Consent Policy

When you call Mile High Home Finders, our AI assistant Leo may offer to text you property details discussed during the call. SMS messages are only sent when you verbally request them during a live phone conversation.

## Opt-In

By verbally confirming you'd like to receive a text during your call, you consent to receiving an SMS with property information at the phone number you're calling from (or an alternate number you provide).

## Opt-Out

You can decline at any time during the call, or reply STOP to any text message to opt out of future messages.

## Message Frequency

Typically 1-2 messages per call, only when requested.

## Example Message

> Hi! Here are the property details from Leo at Mile High Home Finders:
>
> 4521 Zenobia St, Central Park — $625,000
> 4 bed / 3 bath | 2,100 sqft
> Open floor plan, updated kitchen, fenced backyard, close to Central Park trails.
>
> Your showing is scheduled for Saturday, March 8 at 2:00 PM. See you there!
>
> Have questions? Reply to this message or call us back anytime. Reply STOP to opt out.
